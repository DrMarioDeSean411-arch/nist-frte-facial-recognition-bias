What the scatter plot shows: Y-axis position is jittered for legibility — it carries no data value. What matters is color: red = W. African female 65–99 is the worst-performing demographic for that algorithm. Blue = some other group is worst. The vertical band of red persists uniformly from 2017 through July 2025, across algorithms from independent companies, different architectures, and different training pipelines. Three dashed lines mark Gender Shades (Buolamwini & Gebru, 2018), NISTIR 8280 (Grother et al., 2019), and NISTIR 8429 (Grother, 2022) — publications that publicly documented the disparity. The submissions that follow show no visible change in the demographic distribution of worst-case performance.
